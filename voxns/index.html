Webex Calling with Vox

Webex Calling is your complete phone system and collaboration solution in the Cloud!

Webex Calling is a complete enterprise cloud calling and team collaboration solution that offers: all features of unified communications (UC) including voice, video, and collaboration, easy centralized management, and the high security and reliability standards of a global brand you can trust.

Choosing Webex Calling with Vox lets you migrate your telephony to the cloud at your own pace.

Unified experience that is location independent

The integration of applications in the cloud provides a single, consistent calling and collaboration experience for your workforce, remote and mobile users, partners, and customers regardless of how they connect and what devices they are using.

Easy IT management

All collaboration and calling services are integrated via the cloud, which makes administration, lifecycle management and security easier through simple web portals.

Security

Secure calling and sharing with end-to-end encryption is backed by Cisco’s high security standards.

Collaboration tools

Webex Meetings and Webex Teams are included with Webex Calling! Let your workforce use these tools on their desktop or mobile devices to be more productive, from anywhere.

Flexible Cloud migration plan

Flexible cloud, mixed cloud and on-premises deployment options and transition pathways are available, enabling you to transition to the cloud at a pace that is right for your business.
