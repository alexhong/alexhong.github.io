Webex Calling with Vox

Your complete phone system and collaboration solution in the cloud

Webex Calling is a Complete Cloud-based Calling and Collaboration solution that offers all features of unified communications (UC) including voice, video, messaging, meetings, and team collaboration on a monthly subscription model. It is easy to centrally manage and powered by Cisco’s high security and reliability standards.

Why choose Webex Calling?

Industry-Leading Security

Backed by Cisco’s industry-leading security standards, Webex Calling gives you strong encryption, compliance, visibility and control. Inside your own organization, or across company lines, you get a reliable collaboration platform that keeps your data secure.

Cost Savings

Moving your phone system to the cloud reduces total cost of ownership (TCO) and capital overlays for updates and capabilities and lowers maintenance and support costs. Simple monthly per-user licensing minimizes capital investment while supporting scalability.

Flexible Cloud Migration Paths

With flexible cloud deployment options, you can move your phone system to the cloud all at once or in phases. Webex Calling lets you connect your legacy PBX to the cloud so you can access all the capabilities of cloud while protecting your on-premises investments.

Deliver One Unified User Experience Across All Devices and Locations

Integration of applications in the cloud provides a single, consistent calling and collaboration experience for your workforce, remote and mobile users, partners and customers regardless of the device used and no matter their location.

Unlock the collaborative potential of your teams

Webex Calling includes Webex Teams and Webex Meetings with features such as file-sharing, white boarding, video and web conferencing. Use these tools on desktops and mobile devices to empower your teams to be more productive, from anywhere.

Delight your IT team with easy centralized management

All calling and collaboration services are integrated via the cloud, making administration, lifecycle management and security easier through simple web portals.
