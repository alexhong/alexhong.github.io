Webex Calling with VOX

Webex Calling is a Comprehensive Cloud-based Phone and Collaboration solution that offers all features of unified communications (UC) including voice, video, chat, meetings, and team collaboration on a monthly subscription model. Easy to manage and powered by Cisco, Webex Calling is your industry-leading secure collaboration platform in the cloud.

Deliver a Unified User Experience Across All Devices and Locations

Integration of applications in the cloud provides a single, consistent calling and collaboration experience for your workforce, remote and mobile users, partners and customers regardless of where they are located and what device they are using.

Unlock the Collaborative Potential of Your Teams

Webex Calling includes Webex Teams and Webex Meetings with features such as file-sharing, white boarding, video and web conferencing. Use these tools on desktops and mobile devices to empower your teams to be more productive, from anywhere.

Delight Your IT Team with Easy Centralized Management

All calling and collaboration services are integrated in the cloud, making administration, lifecycle management and security easier through simple web portals.

Industry-Leading Security

Backed by Cisco’s industry-leading security standards, Webex Calling gives you strong encryption, compliance, visibility and control. Whether inside your own organization, or across company lines, you get a reliable collaboration platform that keeps your data secure.

Cost-Efficiency

Moving your PBX to the cloud reduces capital overlays for updates and capabilities, maintenance and support costs, and total cost of ownership (TCO). VOX further reduces costs by embedding PSTN into a monthly per-user licensing model that also provides scalability and a single point-of-contact for your PBX network.

Flexible Cloud Migration Paths

With flexible cloud deployment options, you can move your phone system to the cloud all at once or in phases. Webex Calling lets you connect your legacy PBX to the cloud so you can access all the capabilities of the cloud while protecting your on-premises investments.
