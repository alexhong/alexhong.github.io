LeBreeze

LeBreeze is a grey, minimalist theme for Mozilla Firefox and Thunderbird. It was originally conceived and created by Kevin Davis, and based upon another theme called Breeze. After the release of Firefox 0.9, however, Kevin ceased to update the theme, and the task of maintaining it was handed to volunteers of the MozillaZine community, including myself.

Because of continued interest in the theme and the hard work of several individuals, LeBreeze is available today for Firefox 1.0 and Thunderbird 1.0, and is still being updated.
