Monday, August 01, 2005

Moving In

Done. Well, almost. My move from Blogger to Movable Type, that is.

First off, thanks to Spark for generously hosting me on his fast (if somewhat unreliable, heh) G5 server. And of course, thanks to Kass for handling all issues MT-related, including (but not limited to) importing my old entries from Blogger, helping me learn MT’s template tags, installing various plugins, putting up with my sporadic MT-bashing, and generally expending altogether too much time and effort on my crap. I appreciate it.

As should be readily apparent, the outward appearance of this site has not changed. At this point, the only discrepancies between this site and the old one are:

  • The Clippings sideblog has been temporarily suspended until I (“I” as in “Kass”) can figure out how to integrate it into the same MT weblog as my main entries.
  • MT automatically generates the excerpts used in the monthly archive listings, which means I no longer have to come up with stupid one-line blurbs about each entry. Rejoice.
  • Comments are disabled again, simply because I’m too lazy to work them back into the layout. I don’t intend to reenable them, at least not until I decide to completely redesign this place as well, which likely won’t happen for a while.

Okay, no more pointless site updates from here on out.
