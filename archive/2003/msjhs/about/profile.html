Class Length and Units

Students take 6 classes each day. Each class is 52 minutes in length. Each class is 5 units per semester, 10 units per year.

Graduation Requirements

Fremont Unified requires 230 units and seven competency tests demonstrating mastery of the basic skills of reading, writing, math computation and application, English language, study, and location skills. The 230 units must include 4 years of English, 2 years each of math and PE, 1 year each of general science, biology, world history, U.S. history, fine arts or foreign language, and 1 semester each of American government, economics, and health.

Transcripts

On the transcript, the letter (P) in parentheses denotes a college prep course. Honors and Advanced Placement courses are identified with an (HP). The transcript includes two GPAs. The State GPA is all grades earned in grades 10-12 except PE grades. The total GPA includes all grades earned in grades 9-12. Ranking is based upon the total GPA and is not weighted.
